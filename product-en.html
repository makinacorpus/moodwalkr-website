What is MoodWalkR ?

MoodWalkR is an application developed by Makina Corpus that allows to calculate pedestrians itineraries according to their desires. It is possible to connect two points by choosing the fastest route, the cultural walk, the night trip or your desire of nature.

Its special features

Unlike conventional applications, MoodWalkR optimizes the circuit by using sidewalks and crosswalks, taking into account the drop, crossing public places and green spaces.
It is possible to calculate circular course, ideal for sportsmen, tourists and family walks.

Award-winning innovative product

MoodWalkR is the winner of the Booster Nova Smart Cities Digital Challenge. This call for projects is part of an open innovation dynamic in order to create new solutions for the metropolises and their citizens. The selected projects bring new commercial services by combining the Open data of the metropolises and the satellite data.

A Tailored service

When nothing is left to chance
Walking will become a pleasure!

MoodWalkR was designed to explore all routes, big and small. All walks are calculated from the point of view of a pedestrian, taking into account crosswalks, particularly steep areas, and any other potentially interesting surrounding elements. It could, for example, urge you to cross a square or wander through a vibrant area !
