Toulouse

Located in the heart of south-west France, The Rose City is an essential stop that calls for exploration and offers countless ways to appreciate its treasures.
Toulouse has chosen to be guided by MoodWalkR and thus to offer its pedestrians the opportunity to discover the city differently, outside the beaten track.

Montpellier

The Millennial City of Montpellier is creative and festive. It is the Mediterranean, the vineyard, a rich and multiple heritage, traditions and flavors as well as festivals throughout the year…MoodWalkR is the application chosen to offer citizens and tourists an original way to discover the city and its surroundings!
